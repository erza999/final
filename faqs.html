Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best skincare routine?

Our recommended skincare routine involves cleansing, toning, moisturizing, and protecting with sunscreen. For specific skin concerns, consider adding serums or treatments.

How often should I exfoliate?

Exfoliation should be done 1-2 times a week depending on your skin type. Over-exfoliation can lead to irritation and dryness.

Are your products cruelty-free?

Yes, all S Sensational products are cruelty-free and not tested on animals.

Can I use your products if I have sensitive skin?

We offer a range of products formulated specifically for sensitive skin. Always do a patch test before trying new products.

Do you offer samples?

Yes, we offer samples with every purchase. You can also request specific samples at checkout.
